Arduino and AVR Ethernet modules

Published: Fri 25 May 2018
By Tobi Wulff

In misc.

tags: electronics

Full-sized Arduinos such as the Uno together with official or cloned Ethernet shields are easy to use as all the electronics connections and components are well-designed, tested, and the software stack supports these exact configurations.

However, trying to use cheaper modules such as the ENC28J60 or WIZnet W5100 or W5500 can be tricky because the connections have to be made from scratch. On this page I describe my issues while tring to get these modules to work, and document working wiring and software.
